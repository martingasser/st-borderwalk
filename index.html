BORDER WALK

Audio- & Web-Development: Martin Gasser

Border Walk is generative sound composition that uses you geoposition to dynamically adapt to your position and environment.

For a seamless experience you have to prevent your mobile phone from going to sleep mode:

iOs – Settings > Display & Brightness > Auto-Lock > „never“

Android – Settings > Display > Sleep or Screen Timeout > 30 minutes (or longer)
